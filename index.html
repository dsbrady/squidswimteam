he Colorado SQUID Swim Team presents the inaugural Rocky Mountain Autom Gold Rush Short Course Invitational. This is an LGBT-Friendly COMSA-sanctioned, IGLA-recognized swim meet. All Masters teams are welcome to join us for this fun weekend!

Join us at the beautiful Auraria Campus pool in the heart of downtown Denver. We will have both swimming and diving events available, in addition to some fun social activities for our guests starting on Friday night. Hosted housing will be available!

The meet will also include a diving competition, relays, a fun mystery relay, and a special guest master of ceremonies.

Fees

Colorado SQUID swim team is offering several packages for the swim meet.

$50 -This package covers the cost of meet registration, in addition to admission to the social events. SQUID swim team will provide food and beverages for the Opening Social and Awards Picnic, in addition to Appetizers and two free cocktails at Hamburger Mary's.

$25 - Swim Meet Registration Only.

Visitors and guests may also purchase tickets for all social events for $25, and tickets for the awards picnic are available for $7.

Lodging

Visitors to Denver in need of Lodging have several options:

Hosted Housing - Members of the SQUID swim team will provide a place to stay for the weekend. If you are interested in this option, please select Hosted Housing on the registration form. We will email you a hosted housing form to match you up with an appropriate host.

Hotel - The SQUID swim team has selected a centrally located hotel in Downtown Denver within close proximity to the Auraria Campus. Participants and guests can take advantage of special group pricing. If you are interested in a hotel stay, please select Hotel Stay on the registration form, and we will email you reservation and hotel information.

Own Lodging - If you plan on finding your own lodging while in Denver, please select that option on the registration form.
